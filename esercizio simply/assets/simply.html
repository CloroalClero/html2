Focaccia With Caramelized Onions cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Featured in:

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil, plus more for drizzling
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt, plus more
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, plus more
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves, plus more
  • 1 (16-ounce) package refrigerated pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup fresh or frozen cranberries
  • 4 ounces Brie cheese, rind removed and cut into small pieces
  • Coarse sea salt, for sprinkling

For the topping:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon honey

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    2. in a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachement, mix togheter all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
